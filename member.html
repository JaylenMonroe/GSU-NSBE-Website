Become a Member

Looking to join NSEB GSU?

We are thrilled that you are interested in joining the Georgia Southern University (GSU) NSBE chapter, a vibrant community of aspiring Black engineers and STEM professionals. By becoming a member of NSBE GSU, you are unlocking doors to countless opportunities for personal and professional growth, as well as forging impactful connections within the field.

We invite you to discover the many benefits and possibilities that await you as a part of our esteemed organization. Join us as we work together on the exciting path toward academic excellence, professional development, and community engagement.

As a member of NSBE GSU, you will have exclusive access to opportunities such as the Fall Regional Conference (FRC) and the Annual National Convention. These events showcase the passion and dedication of Black students and professionals in STEM, offering countless avenues for career advancement, including job placements, co-ops, internships, mentorships, and valuable networking connections. Not only will these events enrich your academic and professional journey, but they also celebrate the spirit of unity within the NSBE community, making them truly memorable experiences.

Follow the steps below:

To join the Georgia Southern Society of Black Engineers (NSBE GSU) as a member for the 2024-25 academic year, please follow the steps outlined below.

Instructions for the National Society of Black Engineers are also detailed.

Becoming an Active Member

Attend meetings, introduce yourself, and complete the Member Metrics form to get registered and stay involved!

Pay Chapter Dues

To complete your chapter membership, please pay your ($30) chapter dues via Cashapp or Venmo using the button below.

Pay National Dues

Join the official NSBE and pay your National Dues ($15) using your GSU student email. Returning members must also renew their annual membership!

Follow Our Socials

Stay updated with everything NSBE GSU by following us on social media!
