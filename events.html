Our Past Events

First Community Service

Our first community service event was a milestone as we officially came back as a recognized organization on the Georgia Southern campus. On September 2nd, 2023, during the football game against The Citadel, we had the opportunity to contribute to the game day experience at Allen E. Paulson Stadium. Together, we helped with concessions, parking, and other essential tasks that ensured a smooth and successful event. This was a fantastic way for us to kick off the 2023–2024 school year and reaffirm our commitment to giving back through volunteering at GSU football games.

Location: Allen E. Paulson Stadium

Date: September 2nd, 2023

Better World Books Drive

The Better World Books Drive served as the first major incentive event of the NSBE eboard for the 2023-2024 school year. This book drive took place in various locations across campus, running from November 13th to December 1st. Collection boxes were strategically placed throughout Georgia Southern’s campus, making it easy for students, faculty, and staff to donate both new and gently used books of all sizes. The event was a tremendous success, with countless books collected and donated to the Better World Books organization. It provided a meaningful opportunity for our community to give back, helping those in need access books.

Location: Various Locations on Georgia Southern Campus

Date: November 13th - December 1st, 2023

Organization Basketball Tournament

Our organization joined a basketball tournament at the GSU Recreation Activity Center, marking one of the first events during NSBE Week at GSU. This event was a fantastic opportunity to network and have fun with our fellow NSBE members, as well as collaborate with other campus groups like NAACP, BSA, and more. Beyond the game, this event focused on giving back to the community by collecting canned goods for a meaningful cause. Thanks to everyone's participation, we surpassed our goal, collecting over 300 cans! It was a perfect blend of teamwork, fun, and philanthropy, bringing our organization together for a shared purpose.

Location: Recreation Activity Center (RAC)

Date: February 23, 2024

NSBE Resume Workshop

We partnered with the office of Career and Professional Development (OCPD) to host a resume workshop before the Fall 2024 Career Showcase week to prepare our members for the upcoming career fair. This event was a huge success, with many members attending and receiving valuable feedback on their resumes. The workshop covered essential topics like resume formatting, content, and tailoring resumes to specific job opportunities. It was a fantastic opportunity for our members to enhance their professional skills and gain valuable insights from career experts.

Location: Engineering Research Building (ERB)

Date: September 10th, 2024
