About Us

What is GSU NSBE?

Welcome to the Georgia Southern University Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE GSU)!

We are a dynamic and inclusive organization dedicated to empowering Black engineering students, as well as students from other STEM-related majors. Our mission is to inspire academic and professional excellence, develop strong leadership skills, and champion diversity within the STEM community.

At NSBE GSU, we pride ourselves on fostering a supportive network where members can connect, collaborate, and thrive. Through exciting events, impactful programs, and meaningful mentorship opportunities, we aim to create an environment that nurtures growth, builds confidence, and drives success.
