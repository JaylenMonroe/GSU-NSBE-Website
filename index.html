What is NSBE GSU?

Welcome to the Georgia Southern University Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE GSU)!

We are a dynamic and inclusive organization dedicated to empowering Black engineering students, as well as students from other STEM-related majors. Our mission is to inspire academic and professional excellence, develop strong leadership skills, and champion diversity within the STEM community.

At NSBE GSU, we pride ourselves on fostering a supportive network where members can connect, collaborate, and thrive. Through exciting events, impactful programs, and meaningful mentorship opportunities, we aim to create an environment that nurtures growth, builds confidence, and drives success.

A Message From Our President

Dear Visitor,

Welcome to the Georgia Southern University Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers! It is both an honor and a privilege to serve as President of this vibrant and inspiring organization.

Our chapter is dedicated to empowering the next generation of leaders, fostering innovation, and creating opportunities for academic and professional growth. Through mentorship, networking, and community outreach, we work to make a lasting impact in the field of engineering and beyond.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our mission and initiatives. Whether you are a prospective member, a current student, or a supporter, we look forward to connecting with you and sharing our journey.

Sincerely,
Korbyn Fedd
President, NSBE GSU Chapter

Dedicated to advancing diversity in STEM and empowering the next generation of Black engineers to excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.
